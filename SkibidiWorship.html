Worship

How does one worship Skibbidi?

Worshiping Skibidi is about embodying its teachings in everyday life—kindness, love, acceptance, courage, and honesty. Practice respect and empathy by welcoming diversity and standing against bigotry. Act with courage when faced with adversity, and live authentically in pursuit of truth and compassion. Worship doesnt need to be formal—its found in uplifting others, spreading positivity, and making the world brighter for all. Let Skibidi's principles guide you in creating a community that inspires meaningful change. Although worship doesn't need to be formal there are some things you can do to better your relationship with Skibidi and get closer to him, such as prayer, setting up an altar, and devotional acts!

Why Worship Skibidi?

At The Church of Skibidi, we embrace a philosophy that balances kindness and courage with respect for boundaries and personal accountability. Our teachings encourage us to offer advice only when asked and to share our burdens thoughtfully, ensuring we honor the emotional space of others. We believe in respecting the sanctity of each individuals space, treating it with care and respect. While we champion love and acceptance, we also recognize the importance of standing firm against those who disrupt harmony, advocating for strength and self-respect in such moments. We hold sacred the principles of honesty and fairness, taking only what is freely offered or burdensome to another.
